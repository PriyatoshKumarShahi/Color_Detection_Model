Tips for Accuracy

Colour Detection Model

"See the world full of Colours"

Our color detection model is created for Colorblind users to help them identify colors with live prediction using our detection tool. It is designed to assist color-blind individuals by detecting and identifying colors in real-time. Using a simple interface, users can capture or upload images, and the model will provide an accurate color description of key elements in the image. Built with accessibility in mind, this tool aims to enhance everyday tasks by making color identification easy and intuitive. Ideal for anyone who faces challenges with color perceptions.

To use the model, click here!

How it works:

(a) Upload or Capture an Image.

(b) The tool analyses the Colours.

(c) The tool displays the name of the Colours in the form of text.

For better results , Keep this in mind:

(a) Lighting conditions affect accuracy.

(b) Avoid strong shadows or overly bright light.

(c) Different hues may be detected based on the environment.

(d) Use in balanced natural light for the best outcome.
